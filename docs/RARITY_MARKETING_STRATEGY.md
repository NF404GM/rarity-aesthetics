## MARKETING CHANNELS & TACTICS

### 1. INSTAGRAM MARKETING (Primary Channel)

**Content Strategy (40/30/20/10 Rule):**
- 40% Portfolio (before/afters, lash work showcases)
- 30% Education (tips, guides, myth-busting)
- 20% Behind-the-scenes (process, studio, Ashley's story)
- 10% Promotional (offers, workshops, products)

**Posting Schedule:**
- **Feed Posts:** 4-5 per week (Mon, Wed, Fri, Sat, Sun)
- **Stories:** 5-10 daily
- **Reels:** 2-3 per week
- **Best Times:** 9-10am, 12-1pm, 7-9pm MST

**Content Pillars:**
- **Monday:** Motivation Monday - Client transformations
- **Wednesday:** Education Wednesday - Lash care tips, style guides
- **Friday:** Feature Friday - Specific style spotlight, product highlight
- **Saturday:** Behind the Scenes - Process, studio life
- **Sunday:** Self-Care Sunday - Aftercare routines, relaxation

**Growth Tactics:**
- Strategic hashtag use (10-15 per post, mix of sizes)
- Engagement pods with local beauty businesses
- Collaborate with Denver influencers (gifted services)
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- Share user-generated content (with permission)
- Instagram Shopping for products

### 2. GOOGLE BUSINESS & LOCAL SEO

**Google Business Optimization:**
- Complete profile with all services listed
- 50+ high-quality photos uploaded
- Post weekly updates (services, offers, news)
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
- Encourage happy clients to leave reviews (goal: 100+ reviews by end of year)

**Local SEO Strategy:**
- Target keywords: "lash extensions Thornton", "Denver lash artist", "best lashes near me", "custom lash design Denver"
- Create location pages on website
- Get listed in Denver beauty directories
- Build backlinks from local Denver sites
- Optimize for "near me" searches

### 3. EMAIL MARKETING

**List Building:**
- Website popup (10-15 second delay): "Join VIP list for 15% off first product order"
- Footer signup forms on every page
- Post-purchase email capture
- In-studio signup (tablet at checkout)
- Goal: 100+ new subscribers per month

**Email Sequences:**

**Welcome Series (5 emails):**
1. Welcome + discount code (sent immediately)
2. Brand story + why custom lashing (day 2)
3. Style guide + which lashes for you (day 4)
4. Client testimonials + booking CTA (day 7)
5. Product recommendations (day 10)

**Regular Newsletters (2x per month):**
- Lash care tips
- New product launches
- Seasonal promotions
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Client spotlights
- Workshop announcements

**Automated Campaigns:**
- Post-booking: Preparation instructions
- Post-appointment: Aftercare guide
- 2-week reminder: Time for your fill
- Abandoned cart: Product reminder (if applicable)
- Re-engagement: Win-back inactive subscribers

### 4. CONTENT MARKETING (Blog/SEO)

**Blog Post Schedule (2 per month):**

**Evergreen Content:**
- "Complete Guide to Lash Extensions: Everything You Need to Know"
- "How to Choose the Right Lash Style for Your Eye Shape"
- "Lash Extension Aftercare: The Ultimate Guide"
- "Natural vs Volume Lashes: Which is Right for You?"
- "5 Common Lash Extension Myths Debunked"

**Seasonal Content:**
- "Best Lash Styles for Summer/Wedding Season"
- "Holiday Glam: Dramatic Lash Looks"
- "New Year, New Lashes: Fresh Start Guide"

**Local Content:**
- "Best Beauty Services in Denver: A Local's Guide"
- "Top 10 Date Night Spots in Denver (With Perfect Lashes)"
- "Denver Bride's Guide to Wedding Lash Extensions"

**SEO Optimization:**
- Target long-tail keywords (low competition, high intent)
- 1,500-2,500 words per post
- Include images, videos, infographics
- Internal linking to service pages
- Meta descriptions optimized for click-through

### 5. REFERRAL MARKETING

**Referral Program: "Love Your Lashes? Share the Love!"**

**Rewards Structure:**
- Refer 1 friend: Both get $25 off next service
- Refer 3 friends: Get 1 free fill ($50 value)

**Implementation:**
- Digital referral cards (shareable via text/social media)
- Unique referral codes for tracking
- Automated tracking system

- Promote in email signatures, post-appointment communications

**Photography:**

**Corporate Partnerships:**
- Offer corporate packages (team events, employee perks)

## MEASUREMENT & ANALYTICS

### Monthly Tracking Dashboard

**Website Analytics:**
- Total traffic (goal: grow 20% month-over-month)
- Traffic sources (organic, social, direct, referral)
- Bounce rate (target: under 50%)
- Time on site (target: 2+ minutes average)
- Conversion rate (target: 4%+)
- Top performing pages

**Social Media Analytics:**
- Follower growth rate
- Engagement rate (target: 4-6%)
- Reach and impressions
- Top performing posts (save for future content)
- Story views and interactions
- Website clicks from Instagram

**Email Marketing Analytics:**
- List growth rate
- Open rate (target: 25-35%)
- Click-through rate (target: 3-5%)
- Conversion rate from emails
- Most clicked links
- Unsubscribe rate (keep under 0.5%)

**Business Metrics:**
- Total revenue
- Revenue by source (services vs products)
- New clients vs returning
- Average transaction value
- Client acquisition cost
- Lifetime client value
- Booking rate and availability

**Review & Adjust:**
- Weekly: Check daily metrics, adjust social posting
- Monthly: Full dashboard review, identify trends
- Quarterly: Major strategy assessment, pivot if needed
